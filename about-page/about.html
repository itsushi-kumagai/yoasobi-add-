About

What’s yoasobi ?

“Yoasobi” means “night play” in Japanese and means walking around at night. In English, it is written as nightlife. For example, it means playing with a friend or acquaintance at karaoke, a game center, a club or a bar even after midnight. We named foreign tourists visiting Japan to enjoy not only daytime but also night events and fun, and to enjoy nightlife in Japan.

Why did you create this site?

For foreigners visiting Japan to enhance their nightlife We received requests to meet many people and to know not only the famous clubs and bars among tourists, but also spots that only Japanese people can understand.
・ Introducing Japanese event information in English for foreigners　
・ Also providing a place for communication between users by adding SNS functions, You can actually meet the commented user at the event venue.
・ By adding the event save function, you can always watch the events registered in your favorites.
Since I felt that a role of a new platform with various functions was necessary, I made it.
